Welcome

The web has come a long way from simple static pages to a dynamic, mobile-first digital world. This site offers a short and accessible journey through four key topics that define the internet as we know it today.

Whether you're curious about how the web began, how to stay safe online, why mobile-first design matters, or what the internet looks like in Lebanon and the Middle East , there's something here for you.
